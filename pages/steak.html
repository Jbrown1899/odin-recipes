The pinnicle of meats!

Beef steaks are a delicious and safe meat to cook. Steak can be grilled, baked, fried, and eaten raw(from good sources).In this recipe we will pan fry our steak.

Ingrediants

  • Smoked Salt, ground black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Cast Iron pan

Recipe

  1. 24 hours before cooking coat the steak in salt
  2. Dry both sides of the steak with a paper towel and add the ground pepper.
  3. Mince the garlic and coat the steak with a thin layer of garlic
  4. Pre-heat the pan until close to smoking
  5. Add a slab of butter to get a light coat on the pan and then quickly add the steak. (Depending on the fat content of the steak it could be benificial to add another slab of butter onto the steak and baste the meat while it cooks)
  6. Flip the steak periodically until desired internal temperature is reached.
  7. Remove from pan and allow to rest for 10 minutes
  8. Slice and Enjoy!!
