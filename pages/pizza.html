A light appetizer to share with among friends

The word pizza can be dated back to 997 AD Gaeta and some say this was when true culture was born.

Ingrediants

  • 4.5 cups bread flour
  • 1.5 cups warm to hot water
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Pizza Stone

Recipe

  1. Place a pizza stone in oven and pre-heat the oven to the highest setting.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in stand mixer and mix with dough hook.
  3. When mixed slowly add the wet ingredients, save a tablespoon of olive oil for later.
  4. When mixed into a ball (you'll know it's right when your motor starts to sound like it's working harder) remove it and knead lightly with a bit more flour. Put the dough ball into a glass or metal bowl that's been lined with that olive oil.
  5. Fill a large container with about a 1/2 gallon of the hottest water you can. Rest the bowl in that hot water and wait for 1 hour.
  6. After the dough has risen for an hour, cut it in half and roll out each half to make 1 pizza. The ball will make two pizzas.
  7. Use two cutting boards, one for dough rolling with lots of flour and one to sprinkle corn meal onto so the freshly rolled dough can slide right into the oven.
  8. Add desired toppings
  9. cook until desired doneness is achieved
